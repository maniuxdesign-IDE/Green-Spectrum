          Questions about Explore and Map

          What if I do not know an answer?

          Select “Not known” and record where the evidence may be found. Uncertainty is a useful output.

          Should I answer for the whole organisation?

          Use the scope defined during onboarding. A focused business unit, product, service or challenge may produce more useful findings than an overly broad assessment.

          Are the maturity levels a formal score?

          No. They are a structured reflection tool. The quality of the evidence and discussion matters more than the numerical average.

          Can different departments have different maturity levels?

          Yes. Add notes or create separate journeys where significant differences need to be explored.

          Why are Business questions more numerous?

          The Business Empathy section covers organisational strategy, governance, finance, operations, innovation and resilience. Human and Planetary perspectives remain equally important.

          Does Dark Green always mean better?

          It represents a more systemic and regenerative ambition, but the appropriate next step depends on evidence, context, capability and risk.

          When should I use the downloadable canvases?

          Use them when offline reflection, workshop discussion or collective mapping will improve the quality of understanding.
