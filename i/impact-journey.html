PDF Impact Mapping Journey Canvas Landscape canvas for stakeholders, journey stages, key activities, assumptions, system interactions, experience, and ESG impacts.

Section 01

Define the company journey stages.

Name the five stages of the production, logistics, service, or value-chain journey. These become the columns of the impact map.

Section 02

Populate the journey map.

Work across the board visually. Each cell captures what is happening at that stage: activities, unknowns, interdependencies, experiences, and environmental, social, and governance impacts.

Section 03

Turn the map into opportunities, analysis, and results.

Add the patterns you see across the journey. This converts the map from raw information into a decision-ready understanding.

Output

Mapped system understanding and impact priorities.

This output gathers the interlocking activities, functions, problems, impacts, strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and evidence needs.
