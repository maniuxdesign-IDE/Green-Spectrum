Welcome to Green Spectrum

A guided sustainability workshop app for understanding complex challenges, choosing what matters, and turning insight into practical action.

Understand the system

What it is

A practical operating system for sustainability transformation.

Navigation before information

Green Spectrum helps users understand where they are, why the step matters, and what to do next. It is designed to reduce overwhelm, not add another disconnected framework.

Workshop methods in software form

Each activity page turns facilitation methods into interactive digital workflows, so teams can explore, map, prioritise, decide, and plan.

Built for sustainability leaders

The app supports CSOs, ESG teams, innovation leads, consultants, designers, and cross-functional teams moving beyond compliance toward transformation.

How it works

Six pages. Six guided activities. One connected process.

The landing page introduces the system. The next pages gather context, run activities, and turn the selected challenge into a strategy and prototype plan.

  1. 01 Landing Understand the value and process.
  2. 02 Onboarding Capture context, role, maturity, and goals.
  3. 03 Explore and Map Frame the challenge and map the system.
  4. 04 Impact Journey Reveal impacts across a journey or lifecycle.
  5. 05 Sort and Prioritise Choose which problem to tackle first.
  6. 06 Strategy and Prototype Design interventions, plans, and experiments.
