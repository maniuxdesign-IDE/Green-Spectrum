Section 01

Select the problem pathway and desired outcome.

Bring forward the selected Page 5 problems, define the end state, and make the strategic ambition explicit before planning actions.

Section 02

Backcast the strategy into three horizons.

Define immediate, mid-term, and long-term pathways. The page autofills known Page 5 details, then asks for owners, KPIs, approvals, and expected outcomes.

Section 03

Use the Prototyping Wheel to decide what you need to learn.

Select prototype types and learning dimensions. Different prototypes test feasibility, desirability, viability, scalability, adaptability, or system-level impact.

Section 04

Turn the pathway into experiment cards.

Define testing logic, stakeholders, KPIs, measurement methods, resources, ownership, evaluation criteria, confidence, and review dates.

Output

Strategy, intervention plan, and innovation portfolio.

This output pulls together the chosen problem, three horizons, prototype styles, responsibilities, KPIs, success measures, and next learning decisions.
